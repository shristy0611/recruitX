Job Description
Position: Digital Marketing Director
Company Overview
Company Name: Horizon Global MediaLocation: 4-8-10 Ebisu, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo 150-0013, JapanBusiness: Leading provider of integrated marketing solutions, specializing in digital campaigns, influencer partnerships, and data-driven brand strategies for luxury and technology sectors  

Job Responsibilities

Develop and lead omnichannel digital marketing strategies to drive brand awareness and customer acquisition for high-profile clients in luxury and tech industries  
Oversee the creation and execution of paid advertising campaigns across Google Ads, Meta, and LinkedIn, optimizing for maximum ROI  
Leverage CRM platforms (e.g., Salesforce) to enhance customer segmentation, retention, and personalized marketing initiatives  
Manage influencer marketing programs, forging partnerships with regional and global influencers to amplify brand reach  
Utilize analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Tableau) to derive actionable consumer insights and present data-driven recommendations to stakeholders  
Lead a team of 8-10 marketers, providing mentorship, setting performance goals, and fostering a culture Robert of innovation  
Collaborate with creative teams to produce compelling content using tools like Adobe Creative Suite and Canva  
Present campaign performance and strategic plans to C-level executives and clients, ensuring alignment with business objectives


Qualifications
Required

10+ years of experience in digital marketing, with at least 4 years in a leadership role  
Expertise in managing paid advertising campaigns on Google Ads, Meta Ads, and LinkedIn Ads  
Proficiency with CRM tools (Salesforce preferred) and analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Tableau)  
Proven success in influencer marketing and building partnerships to enhance brand visibility  
Strong project management skills with experience leading cross-functional teams  
Fluent in English; business-level Japanese (JLPT N2 or equivalent)  
Ability to work in a multicultural environment with a global perspective

Preferred

French fluency or working proficiency  
Certifications such as Google Analytics Individual Qualification, HubSpot Inbound Marketing, or PCM (Professional Certified Marketer)  
Experience with Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, InDesign) for content creation  
Background in marketing for luxury or technology brands


Ideal Candidate

A strategic thinker with a passion for crafting impactful marketing campaigns that resonate with diverse audiences  
Skilled at translating data insights into actionable strategies that drive measurable business outcomes  
A collaborative leader who inspires teams and builds strong relationships with clients and partners  
Adaptable and culturally sensitive, thriving in a dynamic, international work environment
