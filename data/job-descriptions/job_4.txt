Job Description
Position: Senior Customer Success Manager
Company Overview
Company Name: SkyTech Solutions Ltd.Location: 2-5-3 Nihonbashi, Chuo-ku, Tokyo 103-0027, JapanBusiness: Leading provider of SaaS platforms, including CRM, ERP, and analytics tools, serving enterprises across Japan and the Asia-Pacific region  

Job Responsibilities

Drive customer success for mid-to-large enterprises by ensuring effective adoption and utilization of SaaS platforms (CRM, ERP, analytics)  
Provide strategic consulting to clients, aligning product capabilities with their business objectives to maximize ROI  
Leverage data analytics tools (e.g., Tableau, Looker) to monitor customer usage, identify opportunities, and present actionable insights  
Lead a team of 4-7 customer success representatives, setting KPIs, conducting performance reviews, and fostering professional growth  
Manage key accounts, ensuring high contract renewal rates and customer satisfaction through proactive engagement  
Collaborate with product and technical teams to address client issues and relay feedback for platform enhancements  
Utilize CRM systems (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot) to track customer interactions and maintain accurate account data  
Deliver training sessions and webinars to clients, demonstrating technical features and best practices


Qualifications
Required

6+ years of experience in customer success, account management, or related roles in the tech/SaaS industry  
Proficiency in CRM platforms (Salesforce, HubSpot) and data visualization tools (Tableau, Looker)  
Basic understanding of IT systems, such as cloud-based SaaS architectures or API integrations  
Experience leading or mentoring small teams, with strong organizational and communication skills  
Fluent in Japanese (native level); business-level English (TOEIC 850+ or equivalent) for regional client interactions  
Proven ability to manage client relationships and drive contract renewals

Preferred

Certifications in customer success (e.g., HubSpot, Gainsight) or IT-related fields (e.g., IT Passport, AWS Cloud Practitioner)  
Experience with SQL or Python for customer data analysis  
Background in sales or technical support within the SaaS industry  
Familiarity with agile methodologies for cross-functional collaboration


Ideal Candidate

A customer-centric professional passionate about driving client success through technology and data  
Skilled at translating technical solutions into business value for diverse stakeholders  
A motivating leader who empowers teams and builds lasting client partnerships  
Eager to stay updated on SaaS trends and contribute to product innovation
