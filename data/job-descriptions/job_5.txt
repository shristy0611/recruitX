Job Description
Position: Technical Sales Manager
Company Overview
Company Name: Nexus Solutions Inc.Location: 1-12-9 Hamamatsucho, Minato-ku, Tokyo 105-0013, JapanBusiness: Provider of IT infrastructure solutions, cloud services, and cybersecurity software for enterprises across Japan and Asia  

Job Responsibilities

Lead sales of IT solutions (cloud services, cybersecurity software, and SaaS platforms) to mid-sized and large enterprises, achieving annual sales targets  
Conduct technical consultations with clients to understand their IT needs and propose tailored solutions  
Demonstrate product features and benefits through presentations, leveraging knowledge of IT systems (e.g., cloud architecture, network security)  
Manage a sales team of 4-6 members, setting goals, monitoring performance, and providing coaching to enhance results  
Utilize CRM tools (e.g., Salesforce) to track customer interactions, manage pipelines, and generate sales reports  
Collaborate with technical teams to ensure seamless implementation of sold solutions, addressing client requirements  
Stay updated on IT trends (e.g., AWS, Azure, cybersecurity protocols) to effectively communicate value propositions  
Represent the company at industry events, trade shows, and seminars to generate leads and build client relationships


Qualifications
Required

5+ years of experience in B2B sales, preferably in IT or technology-related fields  
Basic knowledge of IT systems, including cloud computing (AWS, Azure), SaaS platforms, or cybersecurity solutions  
Proficiency in CRM software (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot) and Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint)  
Proven ability to lead or mentor small teams, with strong organizational and goal-setting skills  
Fluent in Japanese (native level); business-level English (TOEIC 800+ or equivalent) for client communication  
Experience in client negotiations, contract management, and delivering presentations

Preferred

Technical certifications (e.g., AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, CompTIA Security+, or equivalent)  
Experience with IT solution sales or presales engineering  
Familiarity with programming or scripting languages (e.g., Python, SQL) for basic data analysis or customization demos  
Bookkeeping or financial analysis skills (e.g., equivalent to 日商簿記2級) for budget management


Ideal Candidate

A results-driven professional with a passion for bridging IT solutions and client needs  
Confident in explaining technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders in a clear, compelling manner  
A collaborative leader who motivates teams and builds strong client relationships  
Proactive in learning IT trends and adapting to evolving market demands
